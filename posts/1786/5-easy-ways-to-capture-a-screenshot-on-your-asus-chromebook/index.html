Tired of explaining to your friends and family how to screenshot on their ASUS Chromebook? Stop worrying and take advantage of these five easy tricks to take screenshots at any time! Screenshots are a great way to capture and share an exact moment in time. Whether you need to capture a vital conversation, record a step-by-step tutorial or just quickly share a funny meme with a friend, screenshots are a versatile and useful tool. With an ASUS Chromebook, capturing a screenshot is quick and easy. Here are five easy ways to capture a screenshot on your ASUS Chromebook.

  1. Taking a screenshot on an ASUS Chromebook is a quick and easy process. By using the keyboard shortcuts, you can capture the entire screen or a specific section of the screen with just a few simple keystrokes. According to ASUS, “you can take screenshots on your ASUS Chromebook with just a few simple keyboard shortcuts”.

  2. To take a screenshot of the entire screen, simply press and hold down the control key and the switch window key, which is located between the full screen and refresh keys. Your ASUS Chromebook screen will dim to indicate the screenshot has been taken. The screenshot will be stored in the “Downloads” folder and can be easily accessed.

  3. If you want to take a screenshot of just a section of the screen, you can use the combination of the control key and the shift and switch window keys. Once you press the keys, your cursor will become a cross-hair which you can use to select the portion of the screen you wish to capture. The same dimming effect will occur as when you take an entire screenshot. The screenshot will be stored in the “Downloads” folder and can be accessed easily.

Step by Step Tutorial on How to Take a Screenshot on an ASUS Chromebook

  1. Shortcut Key: Use the “Ctrl + Window Switcher” keys together to capture a screenshot of your entire screen.
  2. Right-Click & Save: If you need a more precise capture, right-click anywhere on your screen to save only the selected area.
  3. Chrome OS Screenshot Tool: An easy way to take screenshots and record your screen. All you need to do is access the Chrome OS Screenshot tool and you can take full-screen screenshots or even capture specific areas.
  4. Stylus: If you’re using a Chromebook with a stylus, you can use the built-in tool to take screenshots. Just press and hold the “Eraser” button and drag to capture the area you want.
  5. Chrome Extension: If you need more control, you can use a Chrome Extension to capture screenshots and record videos. Just download the extension and you can capture screenshots of your entire screen, the visible window, or any specific region.

Q: What is a Chromebook? A: A Chromebook is a laptop or tablet running the Linux-based Chrome OS as its operating system. The devices are designed to be used primarily while connected to the Internet, with most applications and data residing “in the cloud”.

Q: What is a screenshot? A: A screenshot, also known as a screen capture, is an image taken of whatever is visible on your computer screen. It is often used to capture text messages, webpages, images, or other information that is visible on your screen.

Q: What are the different ways to take a screenshot on a Chromebook? A: There are several ways to take a screenshot on an ASUS Chromebook. You can use the Chromebook’s built-in screenshot tool, an external screenshot tool, or a combination of the two. The built-in screenshot tool allows you to take a picture of the entire screen or a portion of the screen. An external screenshot tool, such as the Chrome Snipping Tool extension, allows you to take a picture of a specific area on the screen.

Q: How do I take a screenshot of my entire screen using the built-in tool on my Chromebook? A: To take a screenshot of your entire screen using the built-in screenshot tool on your ASUS Chromebook, press the Ctrl + Show windows buttons on your keyboard. Your screen will dim briefly to indicate that the screenshot was taken, and the screenshot will be saved in your “Downloads” folder.

Q: How do I take a screenshot of a specific area using an external tool? A: To take a screenshot of a specific area using an external tool, such as the Chrome Snipping Tool extension, first install the extension from the Chrome Web Store. Once installed, click the Chrome Snipping Tool icon in your browser. When the tool opens, select the “Area” option, and then click and drag your mouse over the area you would like to capture. When finished, click the “Done” button and the screenshot will be saved to your computer.
