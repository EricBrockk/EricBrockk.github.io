Take Screenshots on Asus Taking a screenshot on your ASUS Chromebook is easy and convenient. There are two different ways to do it, depending on whether you want to capture the entire screen or just a portion of it. Here’s how to do both:

Capturing the Entire Screen:

  1. Press the “Ctrl” and “Window Switcher” keys together.
  2. The screenshot of the entire screen will be saved automatically to your “Downloads” folder.

Capturing a Portion of the Screen:

  1. Press the “Shift” and “Window Switcher” keys together.
  2. Use the cursor to select the area that you want to screenshot.
  3. The screenshot of the selected area will be saved automatically to your “Downloads” folder.

You can also use keyboard shortcuts to quickly capture a screenshot. To take a screenshot of the entire screen, press the “Ctrl” key and “F5.” To capture a portion of the screen, press the “Shift” key and “F5.”
