  3. Next, connect your Android phone to your computer and copy the .VCF file to it. You can also use an external storage device like a USB drive to transfer the file.

  4. On your Android phone, open the Contacts app and select ‘Import’. Choose the ‘Import from storage’ option and choose the .VCF file you just copied to your phone.

  5. This will start the process of transferring the contacts from the .VCF file to your Android phone, and all the contacts should be successfully transferred.

  6. Lastly, to ensure that the contacts you just transferred are displayed correctly on your Android phone, open the Contacts app and select the ‘Accounts’ tab. Select the Account where you transferred the contacts to and ensure that it is selected to be displayed.
