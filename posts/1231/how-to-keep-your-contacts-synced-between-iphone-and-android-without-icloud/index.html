  3. Utilize Cloud Services: Lastly, you can also sync contacts between your devices using cloud services such as Microsoft’s OneDrive or Google’s Drive. All you need to do is to create a backup of your contacts on either of these services, then you can restore them on any device that has access to the cloud service.
