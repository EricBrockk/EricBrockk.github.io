Next, adjust the available camera modes to get the desired result. You can opt for either Auto or Pro modes, depending on your preference. After that, you can adjust the exposure by either changing the shutter speed or the ISO settings. Shutter speed allows you to adjust the amount of time the camera takes to capture a picture, while ISO is used to control how sensitive the camera is to light.

Last but not least, make sure to adjust the camera’s white balance settings to match the type of lighting condition you’re shooting in. This ensures that colors appear true to life in your photos and videos.
