2. Steps to take a screenshot on Samsung A32 4G#

  1. To start, locate the power and volume down buttons on your Samsung A32 4G. The power button is located on the right edge of the device, while the volume down button is on the left edge.
  2. For Android devices like your Samsung A32 4G, the process to take a screenshot is quite simple: press and hold both the power and volume down buttons at the same time.
  3. When the two buttons are successfully pressed, a shutter sound will be heard, indicating that your screenshot has been taken.
  4. After successfully taking the screenshot, you will see a small preview of it on the top right of the screen, from which you can have access to other screenshot tools such as cropping, adding stickers and drawing on the image.
  5. Your screenshot will be saved in the Gallery or Photos app. You can view, share or delete the screenshot from there at any time.
  6. For a faster and more efficient way of taking screenshots, you can also use the Assistant Menu method. Just swipe down the notification bar, and click on the Assistive Light icon. This will bring up a menu with several options including taking a screenshot.

3. How to edit/customize screenshot taken from Samsung A32 4G#

Once your screenshot has been taken, you can customize it with a range of tools. These include the ability to trim, highlight, crop, and write on the image. To access these features, open the screenshot and tap the Edit icon in the top right corner. This will open the image editor which will allow you to make changes to the image as desired. You can also use the Pen tool to draw or add annotations to the screenshot. Once you are done editing, you can save the image or print it directly from the app.
