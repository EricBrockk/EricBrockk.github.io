2. Types of screenshot capture on Samsung A32 4G#

On Samsung A32 4G, there are two types of screenshot capture available for users, namely: Scroll Capture and Full Page Screen Capture. Scroll Capture allows users to capture a series of screenshots of an entire page or set of pages. This is useful for capturing items such as webpages, lists, and menus. Full Page Screen Capture lets users take a single screenshot of an entire page, including images, text, and other elements. Both types of screenshot capture can be activated by simultaneously pressing and holding the Power and Volume Down buttons on the side of the device. The image of the desired screen will then be saved in the Gallery/Photo Albums folder of the device.
