Step by Step Guide on Turning Off Voicemail on Android

Disable Voicemail on Android This guide will help you easily disable voicemail on your Android phone.

Step 1: Open your phone’s keypad and dial *#61#.

Step 2: This will bring up your phone’s voicemail details and you’ll see an option to “disable voice mailbox.”

Step 3: Tap on the option and confirm you want to disable voicemail.

Step 4: That’s it- you’ve successfully disabled voicemail on your Android phone.
