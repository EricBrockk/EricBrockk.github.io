Step #2: Install WhatsApp on Your iPhone 13 Pro Once you have backed up your WhatsApp data from your Android device, you need to install the app on your iPhone 13 Pro. Step #3: Restore WhatsApp Data on iPhone 13 Pro

Once you have installed the WhatsApp app on your iPhone 13 Pro, you are ready to restore the backup data from your Android device. WhatsApp will now restore the backup data from your Android phone.
