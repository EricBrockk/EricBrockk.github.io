1. Overview of Transferring Contacts from Android to iPhone#

  1. Begin by opening the Contacts app on your Android phone.
  2. Once the app is open, select the option for Export.
  3. From the list of contacts, you can choose which contacts you would like to transfer.
  4. Tap on the Export button near the bottom of the screen.
  5. After selecting export, you can choose to store the contacts via either internal storage or an external SD card.
  6. Finally, connect your Android to the iPhone and you should be good to go.

2. Step-by-Step Guide on How to Transfer Contacts from Android to iPhone#

  1. First, open the Contacts app on your Android device.
  2. Once the application is open, select the option to “Export”.
  3. A list of contacts will then appear. Choose the contacts you want to transfer to your iPhone.
  4. Tap “Export” again to start the transfer.
  5. Select the storage method for transferring your contacts, either the internal storage or an SD card.
  6. After the contacts have been exported, connect your Android device to your computer.
  7. Transfer the contacts onto your computer by copying them from the storage device.
  8. Once the contacts have been transferred to your computer, connect your iPhone to the computer and open iTunes.
  9. Select the option to “Add files to library” and choose the contacts you want to transfer from your computer.
  10. Lastly, sync your iPhone with iTunes and the contacts will now be available on your device.

4. Conclusion#
