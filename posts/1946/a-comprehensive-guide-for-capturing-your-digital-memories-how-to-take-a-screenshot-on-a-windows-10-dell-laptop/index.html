Alternatively, you can also take a screenshot by pressing the “Alt + Print Screen” keys at the same time. This will take a screenshot of the active window and copy it to your clipboard. You can then paste that screenshot directly into an image editor or other software.

Finally, if you want to take a screenshot of a specific region on your screen, press the “Windows + Shift + S” keys. This will open a small “Snipping Tool” window on your screen. You can then select the region you want to take a screenshot of using the mouse. Once you’re done, click on the “Save” button to save the screenshot to your computer.
