Google Drive allows you to store your WhatsApp chat history in the cloud, and it can be easily transferred to your new iPhone. To start the transfer, you’ll need to open the WhatsApp app on your Android device, and then open the Settings menu. From there, you can select Chats > Chat backup > Backup to Google Drive.

Once the backup process is complete, you can open the WhatsApp app on your new iPhone, and select Restore from Google Drive. This will transfer all of your old messages, images, videos, and other data to your new phone.

Another option for transferring WhatsApp messages from Android to iPhone is using iTunes. This option is particularly useful if you’re an iPhone user who has recently switched to a new device. To start the transfer, you’ll need to open iTunes on your computer and connect your old Android device. Then, go to the File Sharing section and select WhatsApp. You’ll then need to select all the files you want to transfer, and click Save to.

Once the transfer is complete, open the WhatsApp app on your new iPhone, and select Restore from iTunes. This will transfer all of your old messages, images, videos, and other data to your new phone.
