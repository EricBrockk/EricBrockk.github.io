3. Steps to Take a Screenshot on a Chromebook#

Taking screenshots on a Lenovo Chromebook can be done easily with three simple steps. To take a screenshot, press and hold the ‘Ctrl’ key and the ‘Switch Window’ key simultaneously; the screen will dim momentarily and a notification will be given indicating that the screenshot has been successfully taken. Once the screenshot has been taken, the user can find the screenshot in the ‘My Files’ folder in the ‘Files’ app which can be found in the taskbar at the bottom of the screen. Screenshots taken with the Lenovo Chromebook are saved in ‘PNG’ format.

In addition to taking screenshots, users may also record a portion of their screen with the Chromebook’s ‘Capture Screen’ feature. This feature can be found in the ‘More Tools’ menu and is represented by a rectangular camera icon. If a user selects this option, they will be asked to select an area of the screen to record. After recording is completed, the user can choose to save the video on their laptop or on Google Drive.

Lastly, users can also take a screenshot of a specific window. To do this, users must open the window they want to screenshot, press and hold the ‘Ctrl’ key, then press the ‘Back’ key. The screenshot of the open window will be taken immediately and can then be found in the ‘My Files’ folder in the ‘Files’ app.

Overall, taking a screenshot on a Lenovo Chromebook is simple and can be done quickly. By following the 3 steps provided, users can easily take screenshots of their laptop or even a single window. With the native ‘Capture Screen’ feature, users can also record a portion of their screen with ease.
