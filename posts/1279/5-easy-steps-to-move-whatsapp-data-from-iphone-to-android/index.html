The second step is to download and install an app for transferring data from iOS to Android. There are several apps available, such as MobileTrans, that you can use for this task. Download and install the app on both the iPhone and Android devices.

3. Backup data WhatsApp di iPhone#

3 Easy Steps to Backup WhatsApp Data on iPhone:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone and click on ‘Accounts & Passwords’, then click on ‘iCloud’.

  2. Tap on ‘iCloud Backup’, then turn on the iCloud Backup toggle.

  3. Tap on ‘Backup Now’ and wait until the backup process is complete. After that, you can safely disconnect your iPhone from the internet. Your WhatsApp data has now been backed up and you can safely move to the next step.
