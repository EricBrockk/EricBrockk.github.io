Update Redmi 9 Android

  1. Check your current version of Android on your Redmi 9. Go to Settings > About Phone > Android Version. Write down your current version of Android.

  2. Check if your Redmi 9 is up to date. Go to Settings > About Phone > System Update. If the update is available, your Redmi 9 will display the latest version of Android.

  3. Download the latest version of Android for your Redmi 9 if it’s not available. Get the new version of Android from the manufacturer’s website and save it to your computer.

  4. Transfer the downloaded version of Android to your Redmi 9. Connect your Redmi 9 to your PC and transfer the Android file to the device.

  5. Install the Android version to your Redmi 9. Go to Settings > System Update. Select the “choose update package” option and select the Android file you downloaded. Your Redmi 9 will then install the new version of Android.

  6. Restart your Redmi 9. After the installation is complete, restart your device so the new version of Android can take effect.

  7. Check the Android version. Go to Settings > About Phone > Android Version to check if the update was successful. You should now be running the latest version of Android on your Redmi 9.
