1. Preparation for Updating Android Version#

In order to update the Android version of Redmi Note 4, the first step is to unlock the device by pressing and holding the power button. After that, open the home page by swiping the screen. From the home page, scroll down from the top of the screen and select the Settings option. Then, select the “About Phone” option located on the Settings page. In the About Phone page, you will find the “System Update” option. Tap on the System Update option and it will check for any available updates for your device. Now, you are ready to update your Android version.

2. Check the Availability of Android Updates#

Once you have unlocked your Redmi Note 4, swipe to open the home page. Then, pull down the notification bar from the top of the screen and select the “Settings” option. Under this menu, you will find the “About Phone” option. By selecting this option, you can begin the process of checking for available updates for your Android version. If an update is available, you will be alerted and you can proceed to the next step. Otherwise, if your device is running the latest version, you will be notified and you can cancel the update process.

3. Download & Install the Update#

  1. First, the user needs to Tap on the Check for Updates option which is placed under the About Phone section. Then, the phone will automatically connect to the Internet and search for any available updates. If an update is available, the user can simply tap on the Download button. Afterwards a pop-up box with the Terms and Conditions will appear. After reading the Terms and Conditions and accepting them, the update will start to download on the device. Once it is finished, the user can tap on the Install button and the update will start to install on the device. Finally, the device will reboot and the update will be complete.

4. Confirm Android Version Update#

After going to the Settings menu, scroll down to the “System & Device” menu option. Here, you will be able to find the option to check for updates. Tap on the option and your phone will search for the latest version of the Android OS. Once it detects any available updates, it will display them on your screen for you to install. Confirm the update and the updating process will start. Wait for the process to complete and you should be able to enjoy the new version of Android OS on your Redmi Note 4.
