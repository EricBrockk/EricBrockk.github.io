Upgrade Android to Pie

  1. Locate the Settings menu on your Android device. To do so, navigate to the App Drawer, look for the Settings icon, and tap on it.

  2. Select the System option.

  3. Tap the Software Update option.

  4. If an update is available, you will see a pop-up window on the screen.

  5. Tap on the Download and Install button.

  6. Your device will start downloading the update.

  7. After the download is complete, the update will automatically install on your device.

  8. Tap the Restart Now button to complete the installation process.

  9. Your device will now reboot and you will have Android 9.0 installed on your device.
