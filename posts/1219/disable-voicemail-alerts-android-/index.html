1. Introduction to voicemail text message alert for Android#

To start the process of turning off your voicemail text message alerts on Android, open the Phone app. You can find it in your app drawer. In the Phone app, tap the menu button and select Settings. In Settings, you’ll need to select Calls, then Voicemail. Scroll down and you’ll see the Voicemail Text Message option. Tap this and you’ll be able to slide the toggle left to disable alerts. Confirm that you’d like to disable this setting and the alerts will be turned off. Keep in mind that this will only disable a voicemail notification alert for your normal phone number and not any other numbers connected to your phone.

2. Steps to Turn Off Voicemail Text Message Alerts#

  1. First, find the Android Phone app on the home screen of your device. Open the app to access the various settings.

  2. After opening the app, select ‘Settings,’ which will open up the various settings available on the phone.

  3. Scroll down and find the option for ‘Calls’ and select it.

  4. After selecting ‘Calls,’ look for ‘Voicemail’ in the list of options and select it.

  5. Upon opening ‘Voicemail,’ scroll down to the ‘Voicemail Text Message’ option.

  6. Tap on the toggle option displayed to the right of the text message option to disable alerts.

  7. To complete the process, confirm your choice by tapping ‘OK.’

3. Benefits of Turning Off Voicemail Text Message Alerts#

  1. Benefits of Turning Off Voicemail Text Message Alerts By turning off voicemail text message alerts, your phone won’t buzz or light up whenever you receive a voicemail message, which results in fewer distractions while you’re working or enjoying time with your family. This allows you to prioritize which notifications you receive without risking missing important messages. Additionally, you’ll save battery life since these notifications don’t require frequent re-checks, as the voicemail will still remain intact until you decide to open it. Furthermore, this will also clear up some room in your messaging inbox, as all notifications from voicemail messages will be eliminated. Lastly, this setting is easy to switch on or off, so you can choose what works best for you.

Q1: What is voicemail messaging alert? A1: Voicemail messaging alert is a notification on your Android phone when someone leaves you a voicemail message. It can be in the form of an incoming call alert, a text message, or an alert sound.

Q2: Why do I need to turn off voicemail message alert? A2: You may need to turn off voicemail message alert if you find it too distracting or if you would like to keep your voicemail messages private.

Q3: How do I turn off voicemail message alert? A3: To turn off voicemail message alert on your Android phone, you should go to your phone settings, select “Calls,” then select “Voicemail,” and finally, within the “Voicemail” settings, turn off the “Voicemail Message Alert” option.

A4: If the above method doesn’t work, you can also try to disable the voicemail message alert through the app that is managing your voicemail messages.
