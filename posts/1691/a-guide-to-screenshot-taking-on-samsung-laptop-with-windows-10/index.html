Step by Step Tutorial to Capture Screen Images on Samsung Laptops

Take Screenshots on Samsung Laptop with Windows 10

Take Screenshots on Samsung Laptop Taking a screenshot on a Samsung laptop with Windows 10 is a simple process that can be very useful for various reasons. Whether you want to show someone how to perform a certain task or illustrate a problem you are facing, a screenshot is a great way to get your point across quickly. This guide will provide you with the steps to take a screenshot on a Samsung laptop with Windows 10.

  1. Press the “Windows Key + PrtSc” on your laptop keyboard. This will capture your entire laptop screen and make a copy of it.

  2. Locate the “Screenshots” folder located in your “Pictures” folder. This is where your screenshots will be saved.

  3. Open the screenshot file. It should be a JPEG or PNG image file.

  4. Right-click on the screenshot and select “Save as” or “Copy” to another location, such as a drive, folder or desktop.

  5. If you wish to make any edits to the screenshot before saving it, you can open it in an image editing program such as Microsoft Paint.

And that’s it – you have now taken a screenshot of your Samsung laptop with Windows 10. Screenshots are a great way to provide visual instructions or show others what you are looking at on your laptop screen. Be sure to save your screenshots somewhere you can find them easily.

Kamu juga dapat mengambil screenshot dan menggunakan berbagai bentuk, seperti free-form, rectangular, window, ataupun full-screen snip. Cara lain yang dapat kamu lakukan adalah dengan memanfaatkan fungsi jaringan jaringan Windows (WIN + SHIFT + S). Fungsi ini akan memasang dan menyebarkan gambar baru, yang kamu dapat menyimpan atau membagikannya.

Terakhir, kamu juga dapat menggunakan perangkat lunak pihak ketiga seperti Lightshot. Dengan Lightshot, kamu dapat mengambil gambar, melakukan annotasi, mengirim gambar, dan lain sebagainya. Lightshot sangat mudah digunakan dan dapat kamu unduh secara gratis.

3. How to take a screenshot with the keyboard shortcut#

  1. To take a screenshot on a Samsung laptop with Windows 10, locate and press the Windows key + PrtScn keys on your keyboard. This can be done by pressing and holding the Windows key and tapping the PrtScn (Print Screen) key once.

  2. A screenshot of the entire screen will be saved to the Pictures > Screenshots folder in your computer’s file system.

  3. To take a screenshot using only the keyboard shortcut, press the Windows key + PrtScn. The PrtScn key can usually be found near the top right of the keyboard. The location may vary from laptop to laptop.

  4. After you press the Windows key + PrtScn, the screenshot will be automatically saved to the Pictures > Screenshots folder. A notification will also appear confirming that the screenshot has been saved.

  5. If you want to quickly open the screenshot you have saved, press the Windows key + R and type “mspaint” then press enter. This will open up Microsoft Paint, where you can view your screenshot.

  6. Once you have confirmed that your screenshot looks good, you can save it to a folder on your computer for easy access later.

4. How to save and share screenshots taken from Samsung laptop with Windows 10#

Once you’ve taken a screenshot with the Windows key + PrtScn keyboard shortcut, it will be automatically saved to your Pictures > Screenshots folder. From there, you can share it with whoever you’d like. To do so, you can right-click the image, select Share, and choose an app to share it with. You can also copy the screenshot to the Clipboard and then paste (Ctrl + V) it into any program you’d like. Additionally, if you’d like to save the image in a different format, you can click Save as in the context menu to save the image as a .PNG, .JPG, or .GIF file.
