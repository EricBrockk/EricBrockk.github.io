Step by Step Guide on Taking Screenshots on Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra

Screenshot Samsung S22 Ultra Guide to Discover Different Ways to Take Screenshot on Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra

  1. Palm Swipe: You can take a screenshot with a quick swipe of the side of your hand across your phone’s display. First, make sure the Palm Swipe feature is enabled in your Advanced Features settings. To do this, open the Settings app, tap Advanced Features, select Motions and Gestures, and then toggle on Palm Swipe to Capture. Now, all you have to do is put the side of your hand facing your display, and then swipe it across the screen to take a screenshot.

  2. Power and Volume Buttons: You can take a screenshot on your Galaxy S22 Ultra with a combination of the Power and Volume buttons. To do this, press and hold the Power and Volume Down keys at the same time for a few seconds. Release the keys when you hear a shutter sound, indicating that a screenshot has been taken.

  3. Bixby Voice Command: The Bixby voice assistant helps to make taking screenshots easier. The assistant can take screenshots with just a few voice commands. To use this feature, open the Bixby Assistant by pressing and holding the Bixby button. Then, say “Hey Bixby” and ask it to “take a screenshot”.

  4. Scroll Capture: Scroll capture is a feature available on Samsung Galaxy phones. It allows you to capture a long page or text thread in a single screenshot. To use this feature, take a screenshot just like you normally would using one of the methods above. Then, tap the Scroll Capture icon located in the options bar that appears after the screenshot is taken. The phone will automatically take a sequence of screenshots and it will be stitched together into one long screenshot.

  5. Smart Select: Smart Select is a feature available on Samsung phones that allows you to take a screenshot of a specific area of the screen. To use this feature, open the Edge Panel by swiping in from the side of your phone’s display. Tap the Smart Select icon, and select a shape. Then, move the shape to the area of the screen you want to capture. When you are finished, tap the Capture icon. Your screenshot will now be saved to your Gallery.

3. Screenshot tools available in the Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra#

The Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra offers several different tools to take a screenshot on your device. One of the simplest aspects of taking a screenshot on your phone is to use the palm swipe feature. This feature allows you to capture a screenshot by simply swiping the side of your hand over the display of the device. To use this feature, go to Settings > Advanced Features > Motions and Gestures and enable the ‘Palm Swipe’ option.

The second tool available in the Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra is a screenshot button. This button appears as an icon at the top of your screen and can be used to quickly capture a screenshot. To activate this button, you will need to enable the ‘Screenshot Button’ option in the Settings > Advanced Features > Motions and Gestures section of your device.

The third tool available is the Smart Capture feature. This feature combines a screenshot and a crop tool in one and is accessed by pulling down the notification panel and finding the Smart Capture icon. When you open this feature, it will launch a screenshot of your phone’s display and you will be able to crop the screenshot to only select the part of the screen that you want to capture.

The final tool available is the Scroll Capture Tool. This feature allows you to capture a screenshot of a full page by scrolling down the page and capturing each section separately. To use this tool, simply open the Smart Capture feature and tap on the Scroll Capture icon. The screenshot will be saved as separate images that can then be pieced together to create a single image of the entire page.
