Taking screenshots on your Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra can be simple if you know the right way. Here are some tips and tricks to help you out:

  1. Use the physical buttons: On your Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra, press and hold the Volume Down and Power buttons simultaneously for about two seconds.

  2. Use Quick Settings: Slide down the notifications panel, then tap the Screenshot icon.

  3. Use Edge Panel: Swipe from the left side of the display and tap the Screenshot icon in the Edge panel.

  4. Use palm swipe to capture: Navigate to Settings->Advanced Features->Motions and Gestures. Toggle on the Palm Swipe to Capture switch. Then, when you wish to take a screenshot, place your hand vertically along the right edge of your Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra. Swipe across the edge of the device to capture a screenshot.

  5. Use S Pen: If you have an S Pen for your Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra, you can use air command to quickly capture a screenshot. To do this, locate the icon for Air Command and then select the Screenshot option.

  6. Use Smart Capture: After you’ve taken the screenshot, tap the Smart Capture icon that appears to crop, share, edit, or draw on the screenshot.

  7. Use Google Assistant: You can use Google Assistant to take a screenshot by simply saying “Okay Google, take a screenshot.”

These are some of the ways you can take a screenshot on your Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra. With these tips and tricks, you should be able to take screenshots with ease.
