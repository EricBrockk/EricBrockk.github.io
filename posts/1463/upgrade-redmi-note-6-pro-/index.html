2. Backing up Data#

Backing up your data is essential before upgrading your Xiaomi Redmi Note 6 Pro to the newest version of the Android operating system. To keep your data safe, you should create a backup of your system files, including any personal data that you have such as photos, contacts, and music. To back up your data, you can use either the built-in backup feature on your Xiaomi Redmi Note 6 Pro or one of the many third-party backup utilities available. For the built-in feature, you can open the Settings app, then tap either ‘About Phone’ or ‘System Updates’, depending on your device. Next, you need to tap ‘Backup & Reset’ and select either ‘Backup My Data’ or ‘Backup Account’. Finally, you will have to choose a destination to store the backup file, which can either be an internal storage space, an SD card, or a cloud-based storage solution. Third-party backup utilities, on the other hand, can be downloaded from the Google Play Store and provide support for a wider range of storage destinations.
