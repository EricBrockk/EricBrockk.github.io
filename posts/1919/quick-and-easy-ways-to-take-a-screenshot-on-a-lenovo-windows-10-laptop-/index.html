  1. Taking a screenshot on a Lenovo Windows 10 laptop is made easy due to its preinstalled Snipping Tool. According to Microsoft, the Snipping Tool “allows you to capture a screen shot, or snip, of any object on your screen” and also offers more advanced options such as delayed screenshots.

Step by Step Guide How to Take a Screenshot on a Lenovo Windows 10 Laptop

Take Screenshots Windows 10 Taking a screenshot on a Lenovo Windows 10 laptop is simple and easy. Here are the steps to quickly capture a screenshot on a Lenovo laptop:

  1. Press the ‘Print Screen’ key on your Keyboard
  2. The screenshot will be automatically saved in the default Pictures folder as a .png file
  3. Open the default Pictures folder and locate the screenshot you just took
  4. You can open the screenshot with any image editing software to manipulate the image

Alternatively, you can take a screenshot of a specific area of the laptop’s screen by pressing the Windows + Shift + S keys together. The mouse cursor will turn into a select icon allowing you to choose the area of the screenshot you would like to capture. Both methods are quick and easy ways to take a screenshot on your Lenovo Windows 10 laptop.

1. Introduction of Lenovo Windows 10 laptop#

The Lenovo Windows 10 laptop is a great device to capture a quick screenshot of your desktop screen. You can capture a screenshot quickly by simply pressing the ‘Print Screen’ key on your laptop’s keyboard. The screenshot will then be automatically saved in the default Pictures folder of your laptop. To locate and view the screenshot, simply open the Pictures folder and find your screenshot image. You can then open the screenshot image with any image viewer of your choice such as Windows Photo Viewer, Paint, Adobe Photoshop, etc.

In addition to taking a screenshot by pressing the ‘Print Screen’ key, Lenovo Windows 10 laptop also lets you take a screenshot with the Windows Snipping Tool. You can open the Snipping Tool by searching in the Start menu and start taking screenshots. Snipping Tool allows you to capture any kind of screenshot with various snip modes such as full screen, rectangular, window, and free-form. Once you have taken the screenshot with the Snipping Tool, you will be presented with an editing window where you can draw, highlight, or add text to your screenshot before saving it to the Pictures folder on your laptop.

You can also take a screenshot on your Lenovo Windows 10 laptop using the Windows Game Bar. This feature comes in handy when you are playing a game on your laptop and want to quickly capture a highlight moment or a game score. To take a screenshot with the Game Bar, simply press the Windows key + G combination on your laptop keyboard and then click on the ‘Yes, this is a game’ check box. You can then click on the camera icon to take the screenshot. The screenshot will be saved in the default Videos folder on your laptop.

Using the Windows 10 operating system, you can also take a screenshot of a single window on your laptop. To capture a screenshot of a single window, press the Alt + Print Screen keys combination and the screenshot will be copied to the clipboard. You can then open your favourite image editor on your laptop and paste the screenshot from the clipboard. After that, you can save the screenshot to the Pictures folder and view the screenshot image with any Image Viewer.

2. How to take a screenshot using the Print Screen button#

When working on a Lenovo laptop with Windows 10 operating system, quickly taking a screenshot of the display can be extremely useful. All you need to do is press the ‘Print Screen’ key on the keyboard, without the need of any third-party applications. The screenshot will be automatically saved into the default Pictures folder, and you can easily locate it in the File Explorer. From there, you can open the image with any image viewer. To further customize the screenshot before saving, you can press ‘CTRL + Print Screen’, which will allow you to paste the screenshot into an image editor.

3. How to take a screenshot using the Snipping Tool#

The Snipping Tool is a quick and easy way to take a screenshot on a Lenovo laptop. This built-in Windows 10 application makes taking and sharing screenshots hassle-free. To use the tool, open the Start menu and type “Snipping Tool” to search for it. Once the application launches, choose the “New” option to get started. You’ll then have the option to take a “Rectangular Snip,” “Free-Form Snip,” “Window Snip,” or “Full-Screen Snip.” You can also use the “Delay” feature to capture a screenshot of a specific window or menu. After making your selection, the Snipping Tool will capture the screenshot and allow you to save it or share it on any social media platform. You can also draw on your screenshot and add text to it using the built-in features. To access the image, open the Pictures folder from the File Explorer. The screenshot will be saved in the selected format. Open it with any image viewer to view the captured image.

4. How to use the Game DVR feature in Windows 10 to take screenshots#

  1. To capture a screenshot using the ‘Game DVR’ feature on a Lenovo Windows 10 laptop, open the ‘Xbox’ application from the Start menu.
  2. Go to the Game bar located in the left pane.
  3. Click on the ‘Take screenshot’ button and a screenshot will be created and be saved in the ‘Captures’ folder.
  4. You can also use the key combination ‘Win + Alt + PrtScn’ to take a screenshot with the Game DVR feature as well.
  5. To find the screenshot, open the folder ‘Documents > Captures’ and locate the screenshot taken.
  6. The screenshot taken is in PNG format, you can open it and edit it with any image viewer.

Q. What is the easiest way to take a screenshot on a Lenovo Windows 10 laptop? A. The easiest way to take a screenshot on a Lenovo Windows 10 laptop is to press the Windows logo key + the PrtScn (Print Screen) key. This will capture the full screen of your laptop and save it in the Screenshots folder of your Pictures library.

Q. How do I take a screenshot of just a single window? A. To take a screenshot of just a single window, press the Alt + PrtScn keys. The screenshot of the active window will be copied to the clipboard, allowing you to paste it in an image editor or other program.

Q. Can I take a screenshot of part of my laptop screen? A. Yes, you can take a screenshot of part of your laptop screen. To do this, press the Windows logo key + Shift + S. Your screen will become dim and a menu will appear allowing you to select the area of your screen you’d like to capture. Once you’ve made your selection, click on the Save button to save the screenshot to the Screenshots folder in your Pictures library.

Q. Is there an easy way to share my screenshots with others? A. Yes, there is an easy way to share your screenshots with others. Once you have taken a screenshot, you can select the Share option from the top menu. From there, you’ll be able to share your screenshot to social media, email, or other platforms, making it easier to share your screenshots with others.

Q. Can I take a screenshot with a keyboard shortcut? A. Yes, you can take a screenshot with a keyboard shortcut on a Lenovo Windows 10 laptop. To take a screenshot of the full screen, press the Windows logo key + PrtScn. To take a screenshot of just the active window, press Alt + PrtScn. For more advanced screenshot options, press Windows logo key + Shift + S.
