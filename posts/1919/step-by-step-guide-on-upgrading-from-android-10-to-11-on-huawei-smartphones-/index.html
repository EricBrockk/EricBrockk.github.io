Step 1: Open the Huawei App Store on your Huawei smartphone.

Step 2: Tap the ‘Tools’ icon and select ‘System upgrade’.

Step 3: Tap the ‘Check for updates’ button.

Step 4: Follow the on-screen prompts to download and install the latest version of the Android 11 operating system.

Step 5: Wait for the installation to complete.

Step 6: Tap ‘Restart’ when prompted to reboot the device.

Step 7: Once your Huawei smartphone has rebooted, it should now be running the latest version of Android 11.

1. Understand the Benefits of Updating to Android 11#

After selecting ‘Tools’, further-tap ‘System upgrade’. On the next screen, tap the ‘Check for updates’ button to initiate the process of checking for a newer version of Android. If Android 11 is available, it will be listed and users just need to tap it to download and install. During the installation process, the device will reboot several times. Once the installation is successful, the device will run on Android 11.

Another way to update Huawei’s models to Android 11 is to flash the firmware manually. To do this, users will need to download the relevant ROM file and flash it via the Huawei HiSuite. Once the flashing process is complete, users will be able to experience Android 11 with its latest features.

Upgrading Huawei devices to Android 11 not only gives users access to the latest features, but it also ensures that existing features are optimised. Security patches are also updated during the process, which can give users peace of mind. Not to mention, the latest version of EMUI is also updated and can bring an improved user experience.

It is recommended to back up data before attempting to upgrade, whether it’s done via the App Store or manual flashing. This is important as the data stored can be lost during the installation process. Similarly, it is important to ensure that the device is connected to Wi-Fi and the battery is at least 50% charged during the installation process.

In addition, users should ensure that the version of their device supports Android 11. This can be checked on the official Huawei website, and users might need to upgrade their device in order to make sure it supports Android 11.

If users encounter any issues during the update process, they can find solutions on the Huawei support page. Additionally, they can post to the Huawei forum with their issue and wait for a response from experienced users.

2. Prepare Your Device#

  1. Open your web browser and visit App Store.
  2. Go to the “Tools” tab and select “System Upgrade”.
  3. Tap the “Check for updates” button on the lower-right corner of the screen.
  4. Wait for the system to search for the available updates.
  5. Once the update is located, tap the “Install” button to proceed with the installation process.
  6. Select the “Install Now” option to start downloading and installing Android 11 onto your device.
  7. The progress bar will update the status of the installation until the completion.
  8. Once finished, reboot your device to finish the upgrade process.

3. Download and Install the Android 11 Update#

  1. Firstly, open the App Store on your Huawei smartphone.
  2. Select ‘Tools’ from the various options displayed.
  3. After that, select ‘System Upgrade’.
  4. Next, tap on ‘Check for updates’.
  5. A page will appear to confirm the update. Tap on ‘Download Now’ to start the downloading process.
  6. Once the download is complete, a notification will appear informing you of successful download. Tap on the ‘Install Now’ button to begin the installation process.
  7. Now keep your device connected to the internet and wait for the system to upgrade.
  8. After the upgrade is finished, a message will be displayed informing you of completion. Tap on the ‘OK’ button to finish the upgrade process.

4. Ensure Required Apps are Up-to-Date#

  1. Visit the App store, then choose Tools, and select System upgrade.
  2. Tap the Check for updates button and follow the on-screen instructions to download and install Android 11.
  3. Wait until the process is finished.
  4. After successful the installation, make sure to check if all the required apps are up-to-date.
  5. To upgrade, open the app and choose the “update” option.
  6. If the latest version is available, click “update” and wait for the process to complete.

Q1: What is the process for upgrading from Android 10 to Android 11 on Huawei smartphones ? A1: The process for upgrading from Android 10 to Android 11 on Huawei smartphones is fairly straightforward. First, make sure you have the latest Huawei software version installed. You can update your software via the Settings menu, or you can manually download the latest version from the Huawei website. Once you have the latest version of the software, you can go to the System Update section of the settings menu and select the option to upgrade to Android 11. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the upgrade process.

Q2: What should I do if the upgrade process fails ? A2: If the upgrade process fails, it could be due to various reasons such as an insufficient storage capacity, lack of a stable internet connection or an incompatibility issue with the current software version. In such cases, you can perform a factory reset of your device. To do this, go to the Backup and Reset section of the settings menu and select the option to Factory Reset your device. After the reset is complete, you can try to upgrade your device to Android 11 again.

Q3: What type of data will be lost during the upgrade process? A3: During the upgrade process, any data that is not backed up or stored in the cloud will be lost. This includes photos, videos, music, documents, and other files. It is recommended that you backup any important data before you start the upgrade process.

Q4: What precautions should I take before beginning the upgrade process ? A4: Before you begin the upgrade process, it is recommended that you charge your device to at least 50% battery level. Also, make sure you have a stable internet connection as this will ensure the upgrade is successful. Additionally, it is also recommended that you backup any important data before you start the upgrade process.

Q5: How long does it take for the upgrade process to be completed? A5: The time taken to complete the upgrade process depends on various factors such as the size of the update, your internet connection speed, and your device’s hardware configuration. However, it usually takes about 10-15 minutes to finish the upgrade process.
