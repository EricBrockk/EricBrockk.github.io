Resetting your Android TV Box back to factory settings is necessary if you are facing issues related to the applications or the device itself. To reset your Android TV Box, the first thing you need to do is access the main menu. From the home screen, open the settings. In the settings menu, you will find a section called Backup & Reset. Select this option and then look for Factory Data Reset. This is where you will have to choose Reset Device followed by Erase Everything. Upon confirmation, your Android TV box will start resetting itself.

Once the resetting process is completed, your TV box will restart and you will be able to use it again. You will be able to access the same apps, websites and movies without facing any issues. 2. How to Reset Your Android TV Box#

  1. First, go to the Home Screen by pressing the Home button on the remote control.
  2. Secondly, open the Settings menu.
  3. You should then select the “Backup & Reset” option.
  4. After that, you can choose the “Factory Data Reset” option.
  5. When the reset option is selected, a message will appear asking you to confirm this feature, select “Reset Device” to continue.
  6. Once you confirm, the Android TV box will begin to reset and will take some time to complete.
  7. The reset process is complete when you see the “Erase Everything” option.
  8. After successfully resetting, your Android TV Box will restart and you are ready to use it again.

Resetting your Android TV box to its factory settings can provide several advantages. Firstly, it can help you free up some space as it will delete all of the contents you have stored on the device. 4. Final Notes#

It is important to note that resetting your android TV box back to factory settings will erase all personal data, files, settings, and installed applications. Before resetting, please always back up your files and settings to a safe place.

It is also important to make sure that your power supply is stable and the device is adequately charged. Any sudden power loss during the reset process might damage your device.

You should also disconnect any external devices from the TV box before resetting. Once the reset is complete, reconnect any external devices to the TV box.

While a factory reset will not affect the installed apps, user settings such as available channels and user account information will be reset to the factory default. Therefore, please remember to sign in to your account again to access any purchased content.

Finally, make sure that your TV is connected to the internet. The factory reset process will not work if your TV box is not connected to the internet. After resetting, you should also update your device to the latest version in order to take advantage of the latest features and to ensure the best performance.
