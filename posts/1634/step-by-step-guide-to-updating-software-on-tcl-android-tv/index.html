Step by Step guide to Update TCL Android TV

  1. Firstly, make sure your internet connection is active and that your TV is connected to the same network as whatever device you are using.

  2. On the home screen, go to the Google Play Store and select the magnifying glass to search for “TCL Up Center”.

  3. Install the TCL Up Center app if it does not come pre-installed.

  4. Once installed, open the Up Center app and select the “Software Update” option.

  5. Your TV will then search for updates. When it finds one, it will prompt you to install the update.

  6. Select “Yes” to begin the update and keep your TV connected to the internet to allow it to download the update.

  7. Once the update has been downloaded, the TV will ask you to restart. Select “Yes” to restart, and the update will be applied.

  8. After the restart is complete, your TV should now be up to date.

1. Overview on Updating TCL Android TV Software#

TCL Android TVs come with an Android Operating System, meaning that users can easily download and update applications through Google Play Store. Before proceeding with any software update, it is important to ensure that the internet connection is active and that the TV is connected to the same network as the device used. After which, users can go to Google Play Store and search for TCL Up Center. This app will provide all necessary information about the current software version installed, and will enable users to update it. After installation, users can proceed to open the app and select the Software Update option to begin the process. It is advisable to keep the device powered on throughout the entire software update process in order to complete it successfully.

2. Check Device Software Version#

This guide will explain the steps on how to update the software version on your TCL Android TV. Firstly, make sure your device has an active internet connection. Make sure your TV is connected to the same network as the device you are using for the update. Then, you will need to download the TCL Up Center app from the Google Play Store. After it has been downloaded, open the app and select Software Update. The app will check if there is a newer version of the software available. If there is an update available, follow the on-screen instructions to update the software. Once the update is completed please restart your TV to ensure the update is applied successfully.

3. Download and Install Latest Software Version#

Once the software update is available, Up Center will show the prompt to update the TV software. A confirmation box will appear, tap on OK to start the download process. Please note that the download process may take several minutes depending upon the size of the file. After the download process is completed, the TV will be updated to the latest version. To discontinue with the software update process, press the Back button on the TV remote.

4. Troubleshooting#

If problems occur, please restart your TV and device used before trying again. If the update is not available to your TV, contact TCL Support. Please note that the TV requires a stable internet connection to be able to update itself. If you cannot update the TV directly, download the update file and copy it to your USB drive. Insert the USB drive into the TV and run the update software. If the update is still not performing, contact the store or manufacturer.
