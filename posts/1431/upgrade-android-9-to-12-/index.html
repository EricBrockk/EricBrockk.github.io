2. Prepare Your Device before Updating the OS#

  1. Before performing an Android OS update, make sure your device is compatible with the new update. To check, go to the device’s Settings and select About Device.
  2. Disconnect your device from any cables and turn it off.
  3. Make sure you have enough free space for the update by checking your storage. You can also transfer some files to an external hard drive or cloud storage.
  4. Take a complete backup of your device which includes contacts, photos, videos, and all your other files.
  5. Charge your device up to 50% before starting the update process. This is particularly important if you plan to update your OS without connecting to a power source.
  6. Enable Wi-Fi and check that your network connection is stable.
