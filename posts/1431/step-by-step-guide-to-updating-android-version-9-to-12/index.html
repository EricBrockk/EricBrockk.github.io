3. Step-by-Step Guide to Updating Android version 9 to 12#

  1. First, make sure to back up your device data. It is important to back up your phone as the update will erase any locally stored data.
  2. Second, check that your phone has more than 50% of battery life. If it does not, make sure to charge it until it reaches the desired percentage or above.
  3. Third, check for available updates by opening the Settings app and tapping on System > Advanced > System update. Select the “check for update” option and follow the on-screen instructions.
  4. Fourth, install the update, if available. This may take a few minutes, depending on your device and the update size.
  5. Fifth, reboot your device. This is important to ensure that the update is successfully installed and working.
  6. Lastly, once your device is restarted, open the Settings app again and check the Operating System version. This should now be updated to version 12.
