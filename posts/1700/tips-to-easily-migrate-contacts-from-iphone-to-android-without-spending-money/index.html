Here are some simple tips to help you migrate contacts from your iPhone to an Android device for free:

  1. Create a .vcf file: You can easily create a .vcf file in the contacts app on your iPhone. Just open the app, select the contacts you want to transfer, tap the Share button, and tap “Export vCard.”

  2. Send the file to yourself via email or a cloud storage service: Once the file is created, you can send it to yourself via email or a cloud storage service like Google Drive or Dropbox.

  3. Move the file to your Android phone: Now, transfer the file to your Android phone either by downloading it from your email or cloud storage service or by using a USB cable to transfer the file from your computer to the Android device.

  4. Import the contacts: On your Android phone, open your contacts app and select “Import.” Next, select the file you’ve just transferred from the list of options. Your contacts will now be imported into your contacts list.

These simple steps will help you migrate contacts from an iPhone to an Android without spending money. However, if you need additional help, you can always seek assistance from an IT technician or a customer service representative from your device manufacturer.
