A Step-By-Step Guide on How To Transfer Contacts From iPhone to Android for Free

Are you fed up with copying your contacts one by one from your iPhone to your Android? Well, worry no more! This blog post will be your ultimate guide on how you can easily transfer your iPhone contacts to your Android device for free in no time. Are you looking for a way to transfer contacts from your old iPhone to your new Android device without shelling out a single cent? Look no further! This comprehensive guide walks you through the steps to move all of your contacts from iPhone to Android, with zero cost involved. So let’s get started!

  2. Fortunately, the process of transferring contacts from an iPhone to an android device has been made easy, thanks to the presence of a variety of free applications that allow you to do this. For example, Google Contacts allows you to quickly and easily sync all your contacts from your iPhone to your Android device.

  3. According to an expert in mobile phones, “transferring contacts from an iPhone to Android is simple and straightforward. All you have to do is download an application which allows you to sync your contacts, and follow the step-by-step guide to transfer your contacts”. This means that it no longer has to be a complicated process.

Transfer contacts iPhone Android free Step 1: Open the Contacts app on your iPhone. Step 2: Click on Groups in the upper left-hand corner. Step 3: Select All Contacts. Step 4: Click on the Share button in the lower left-hand corner. Step 5: Select Mail or Message and choose how you’d like to send the contact list. Step 6: Open up the email you sent yourself on the Android device and click on the link to download the file. Step 7: Download the file to your phone’s internal storage. Step 8: Open the Contacts app on your Android. Step 9: Select Import/Export in the app’s menu. Step 10: Select Import from storage and select the file you just downloaded. Step 11: Select which contacts you’d like to import and then select Done to import them to your Android device.

1. Introduction#

To start, unlock the iPhone and open the Contacts app. Then, select ‘Groups’ at the top of the screen and click ‘All Contacts.’ Once all of the contacts are displayed on screen, click ‘Share’ to bring up the sharing options. Choose ‘Mail’ or ‘Message’ and enter the recipient’s email address or phone number. This is where you need to enter the email address or phone number attached to your Android device. Finally, hit ‘Send’ to send the contacts in a VCF (vCard) file. On your Android device, open the sent email or message and download the attached VCF file. The contacts list should then automatically be imported to the Android device.

2. Transferring Contacts with iTunes#

  1. Connect your iPhone to your computer using a USB cable. You will need iTunes installed on your computer.
  2. Open your phone’s Contacts application.
  3. Select the group of contacts you want to export. For example, if you want to export all your contacts, select “All Contacts”.
  4. Click the “Share” button. The button is usually found in the bottom of the contact list.
  5. A selection of share options will be given. Choose either “Mail” or “Message”.
  6. Enter the email address of the Android device in the To line and send the file.
  7. On the Android device, go to the sent email and download the vCard file, which contains the contacts you sent from your iPhone.
  8. Finally, open your Contacts application on the Android device and select the import option, finding and downloading the vCard file that was sent from your iPhone.

3. Transferring Contacts with Google Account#

First, you will need to have a Google Account. If you don’t have one, sign up for a new Google Account on the Google website. After that, open Contacts from the Home Screen of your iPhone. On the Contacts page, select the groups of contacts that you would like to transfer. To transfer all contacts, select ‘All Contacts’. After that, click the ‘Share’ option and select ‘Mail/Messages’. Now compose a new email or message, and send it to the email address that is connected to your Google Account.

Next, open the email on the Android device that is associated with the Google Account. Download the attached .vcf file. Now, open Contacts on the Android device, select More, select Import/Export, select the .vcf file, and click Import. All your contacts should now be transferred to the Android device.

You should also make sure that the contacts are synced with the Google Account. To do this, open Contacts on the Android device, select More, select Settings, select the Google Account that the contacts were transferred to, and check the Sync Contacts box.

If you would like to transfer contacts from Android to iPhone, the process is similar. First, open Contacts on the Android device. Select More, select Import/Export, select Export and choose where to save the .vcf file. After that, attach the file to an email and send it to your iPhone. Open the email on the iPhone, download the file, and then open Contacts. Select More, select Import/Export, select the .vcf file and click Import. The contacts should now be transferred from the Android device to the iPhone.

4. Conclusion#

After you receive the email, open the attachment on your Android device. It should contain the contacts you have exported from your iPhone. To add these contacts to your Android device, find the Import Contacts option in the Contacts app. This should work for most of the Android devices and will move all of your contacts from the mail attachment to your Android device with ease. If you are having trouble finding this option, you can refer to the complete user manual of your device for more detailed instructions.

Once you have imported the contacts from the mail attachment, you can also manually edit the contacts or delete them if you wish. You can also search for a particular contact and add additional information such as email or phone numbers as needed.

All the contacts you have exported from your iPhone should now be visible in the Contacts app on your Android device. This process is really simple and takes very few steps to complete. With this method, you can easily transfer your contacts from your iPhone to your Android device for free.
