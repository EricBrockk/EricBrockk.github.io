  2. Transfer Photos Using Your Google Account - A great way to transfer photos from your Android device to your iPhone is by using your Google account. This will allow you to transfer your photos from your Android device to your iPhone in just a few simple steps. First, open the Google Photos app on your Android device. Then, make sure both your Android device and your iPhone are connected to the internet and that they are both on the same Wi-Fi network. Select the photos you want to transfer to your iPhone and tap the “Share” button. Enter your Google account information on the next screen and tap “Share.” Finally, open the Google Photos app on your iPhone, select the “Shared” tab and tap the “Download” button to transfer the photos you selected from your Android device to your iPhone.

Open the app on both devices and select the photos you want to transfer. Then, follow the on-screen instructions to transfer the photos from your Android device to your iPhone.

  2. Download the Google Drive app onto both your Android device and your iPhone. Sign in with the same Google account on both devices.
  3. On your Android device, open Google Drive and select your photos. Tap the ‘share’ icon, which looks like an upwards arrow.
  4. Select ‘add to Google Drive’, and they will be added to your Google Drive.
  5. Open Google Drive on your iPhone. Select the photos you want to transfer, tap the ‘share’ icon and select ‘Save’ or ‘Download’ to save the chosen photos to your iPhone.

Q: How Do I Transfer Photos from Android to iPhone After Setup? A: To transfer photos from your Android device to your iPhone after setup, simply follow the steps outlined below:

  3. Tap the Share icon.
  4. Select “Send to iPhone”.
  5. Connect your iPhone to your Android device via Bluetooth.
  6. Select “Accept” on your iPhone when prompted.
  7. The selected photos will now be transferred to your iPhone.
