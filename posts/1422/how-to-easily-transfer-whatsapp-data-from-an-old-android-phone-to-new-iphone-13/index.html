Step 2: Download the Move to iOS app. On your iPhone 13, download the Move to iOS app from the App Store. This app is designed to help you move data from your Android phone to your new iPhone.

Step 3: Connect your phones. Connect your old Android device and your new iPhone 13 to the same Wi-Fi network. Then, open the Move to iOS app on both devices.

Step 4: Follow the on-screen instructions. Once you’ve connected both of your phones to the same Wi-Fi network, you’ll be prompted to enter a security code displayed on your iPhone 13.
