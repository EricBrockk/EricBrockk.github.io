- Capturing a Screenshot on Surface Pro 7#

To take a screenshot on your Surface Pro 7, press and hold down the Windows logo button and the volume-down button for 2 seconds. You will then hear a shutter sound to indicate that the screenshot has been successfully taken. This screenshot will then be saved in the Pictures/Screenshots folder of your device. You will also get a Screenshot Captured notification from the Action Center, which can be seen in the lower-right corner of your screen. You can also check the notification tray of your Surface Pro 7 to view the screenshot that you have just taken. The screenshot can then be shared, edited, or deleted as desired. To share the screenshot, simply go to the Pictures/Screenshots folder and select the picture you wish to share. You can then choose where you want to share the picture, such as on social media, through a messaging app, or to another device. To edit the screenshot, open the picture in the Photos app on your Surface Pro 7 and use the many tools and effects available to customize the image as desired. Lastly, you can delete the screenshot by going to the Pictures/Screenshots folder and selecting the delete option. You can also delete the screenshot directly from the notification tray.
