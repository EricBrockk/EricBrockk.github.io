Guide To Transfer Photos Between iOS and Android Devices Using Bluetooth

Transfer Photos via Bluetooth Step 1: On your iPhone, open the Photos app and select the photos you wish to send. Tap the Share icon. Step 2: Tap the Bluetooth icon in the share sheet. Step 3: On your Android device, enable its Bluetooth connection. Step 4: Switch to the Photos application on your Android device. Step 5: On your iPhone, tap the Bluetooth connection you want to connect to, and tap Send. Step 6: On your Android device, the photos will show up in the Photos app. Go ahead and save them to your device. Step 7: Once all the photos have been received, tap the Disconnect icon on your iPhone. Step 8: Finally, disable the Bluetooth connection on both devices.

1. Understand Your Devices’ Bluetooth Versions#

To transmit photos from your iPhone to your Android device, it is important to understand the Bluetooth versions on both devices. If you have different Bluetooth versions on either device, it could result in slower transmission speeds or a failed connection. Therefore, you should check the Bluetooth version of both devices before attempting to pair them. To do this, access the settings menu on both devices and look for the Bluetooth version number. Once you have ensured that both devices have compatible Bluetooth versions, you can proceed to connect them.

2. Pair Your Devices#

After selecting the photos to transmit on your iPhone, turn on Bluetooth on your Android device. On your Android device go to the Photos app and select the Bluetooth button. Now, the two devices will pair and a prompt will be displayed on your iPhone. Tap Allow to accept the connection and then tap Send to transmit the photos on your iPhone to your Android device. Once the photos are sent, the prompt will automatically disappear and the pairing between the two devices will end. Your photos will now appear in your Android Photos app.

3. Select And Transfer Photos#

To complete the easy process of transferring photos from an iPhone to an Android device, simply open the Photos app on the iPhone and select the photos that you would like to transmit. Once these have been chosen, press the share icon and then select the Bluetooth icon within the share sheet. To make sure the photos are transferred correctly, ensure that the Bluetooth connection on the Android device is active. From there, switch to the Photos app on the Android device, click on the Bluetooth connection that you wish to connect on the iPhone, and then press Send. The photos should then be quickly and easily transferred to your Android device. For a more secure transfer, try using a cloud-based photo storage service.

4. Check your Device for Transferred Photos#

Once the Bluetooth connection has been established and your devices are connected, you need to open the Photos app on your iPhone, select the photos you want to send and then press the Share icon. After that, tap on the Bluetooth icon on the share sheet. On your Android device, activate the Bluetooth connection and switch to the Photos app. Then, tap on the Bluetooth connection that you want to connect from your iPhone and press Send. Finally, you can check your Android device to see if the photos have been transferred successfully.

Q. How can I effortlessly transmit photos from iPhone to Android via Bluetooth? A. You can effortlessly transmit photos from iPhone to Android via Bluetooth with these simple steps:

  1. On your Android device, turn on Bluetooth.
  2. On your iPhone, open the Photos app and select the photos you want to transfer.
  3. Tap the Share button.
  4. Tap Bluetooth.
  5. Select your Android device from the list.
  6. Enter the PIN that appears on your Android device.
  7. You will see a confirmation on your Android device when the photos have successfully been transferred.
