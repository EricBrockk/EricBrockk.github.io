1. Prepare your devices#

To migrate contacts from an iPhone to an Android device without losing any data, it is important to first prepare your devices. This includes connecting your iPhone and Android device to a computer, downloading and installing the iSkysoft MobileTrans app, and launching the app to select “Phone to Phone Transfer” from its main window. After these steps have been completed, you will be able to transfer the contacts from your iPhone to your Android device, ensuring that all the contact information is kept intact. Additionally, make sure that both devices are connected to the same Wi-Fi network prior to initiating the transfer process. This will help the transfer progress more quickly and avoid any interruption due to a weak internet connection. Furthermore, it is recommended to have both devices completely charged prior to beginning the transfer process, as this will help to ensure that the transfer is successful. Properly preparing your devices is an essential step when migrating contacts from an iPhone to an Android device without losing any data.

2. Transfer contacts using Google account#

This app will connect the iPhone to the computer. Then, select “Source” and “Target” devices. It will detect the connected iPhone as the source and the Android device as the target device. Then, select the data type “Contacts” from the list of available items. Select “Start Transfer” to begin migrating the contacts from the iPhone to Android device. During the transfer process, the data will be encrypted for secure transfer. After the transfer is completed, you will get a notification that all the contacts have been transferred to the Android device. You can also verify the contacts on the Android device to ensure all the contacts are transferred. Furthermore, you can also backup your contacts to your Google account or another cloud storage service to ensure they are safe.

3. Transfer contacts using third-party software#

Firstly, connect your iPhone to the computer by using a USB cable. After that, you should download and install iSkysoft MobileTrans app from its official site. Once the app is launched, select “Phone to Phone Transfer” from the main window. This process is designed for users to transfer data from one phone to another phone. On the next window, select “iOS” on the left side and “Android” on the right side. You will then be required to enter a code for both phones for the data transfer. After that, click on “Start Copy” option to start the data transfer process. During the process, you will be able to see a prompt of the transferring process. Once complete, click on “OK” to end the data transfer process. Then, you can disconnect both phones from the computer. Your contacts should now be migrated from iPhone to Android.

4. Verify the transferred contacts#

After you connected your devices and selected the transfer type, you will see the contacts from your iPhone listed on the left side and contacts from the Android phone on the right side. You can select the contacts you want to migrate and click Start Transfer. Once the process is done, you have to verify that the contacts have been migrated successfully. In the process, all your contact details such as contact numbers, pictures, contact notes, and email address are transferred without any data loss. On the iPhone device, open the Contacts app and check to make sure all your contacts have been transferred to the Android phone. On the Android device, open the contacts app and confirm that all the contacts have been moved.
