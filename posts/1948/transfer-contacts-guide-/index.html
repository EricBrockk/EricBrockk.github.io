  3. To make the switch from iPhone to Android even easier, experts suggest using a third-party app such as Move to iOS. Step 1: Connect iPhone to Your Computer

To get started, you’ll need to connect your iPhone to your computer. Use the USB cable that came with your device to connect it to your computer.

Step 2: Export Contacts from iPhone

Once your device is connected to your computer, open iTunes. From the menu bar at the top of the iTunes window, select File > Devices > Transfer Purchases from [device name].

Once your contacts have been transferred, click the File menu and select Library > Export Playlist. Select an output location for your contacts and then click Save.

Step 3: Import Contacts to Android

Connect your Android device to your computer. Open your Android device’s storage and then select the folder named Contacts. Copy the contacts file you saved in Step 2 to this folder.

Step 4: Sync Contacts to Android

Once you have copied the contacts file to your device, launch your phone’s Contacts app. Select the Menu button and then select Import/Export contacts. Select Import from storage and then locate and select the contacts file you transferred.

Your contacts will now be imported to your Android device.
